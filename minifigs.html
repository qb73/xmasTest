WWII Minifigs

I want minifigs because of my interest in stop-motion. However these aren't just for that, these (for me, anyway), I like to collect them because of me organizing their guns and equipment to equate to the real life counterparts used in the war. I want to get more germans, and allied soldiers, as aforementioned, being displayed by their real life organization.

These items are very different in quality and visuals, so there isn't really a particular price to look at. But from what I want, they could range in cost (in individuals or in groups) from ~$12 to ~$65.
