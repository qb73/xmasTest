Entrenching Tool

Similar to the WWI gear, I want a variety of other things (like weapons) for the feeling of a solider in the flesh, with full kit. Particularly an entrenching tool. At least in WWI, every standard soldier would carry one. Whether in one, or two pieces, consisting of the handle and the head.

An entrenching tool isn't necessarily just for looks in this case, it also has a pratical value as being an entrenching tool, which can be used to re-shaping landscape for whatever purpose. For this item, for both pieces (at least for the British one) would cost around ~$100.
