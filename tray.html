Dice Tray

I want a dice tray because I would like to have something that is practical, yet big and solid enough for dice space. I got a set of wargame rules called "Crossfire" last year, which require miniatures, terrain, and most importantly, plenty of dice. Among those mentioned, I have a habbit of accidentally letting dice onto the ground, thus I'd like to prevent that.

For this item, I have a specific model in mind. It isn't very costly, but gets the job done, whilst also looking good and large enough for many dice. Particularly, the one I want to get is almost a 1' wide × 1' long, plenty of space to roll in, a decent protection of the surface it's on, and a pad to help preserve dice. This costs around ~$30.
